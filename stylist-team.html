Meet our stylists

Monica Benes

Hi there, I’m Monica, and I’ve been a hair stylist at Brand Salon for six years. Originally born and raised in New York City I moved to Asheville, NC in 2014 looking for a new adventure. Working at Hair Salon has been my favorite adventure so far. I love building strong connections with my clients and helping them express their personalities through their appearance!

Eric Diaz
